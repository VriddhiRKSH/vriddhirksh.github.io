About Me

Two passions,
one purpose

I'm Swati Tyagi — a yoga teacher and web designer based in the sacred city of Rishikesh, nestled in the Himalayas. Originally from Wave City, Ghaziabad, I found my home in the mountains and my calling in stillness.

Through yoga, I help students reconnect with their breath, body, and spirit. Through design, I help businesses create meaningful digital experiences. Both practices share the same philosophy: intentionality brings clarity.
